PCB Assembly · Small Batch · Quick Turn

Since 2019, we have been designing, engineering and manufacturing small batch assistive technology that supports severely disabled veterans throughout the USA. Getting small quantities of printed circuit boards assembled domestically in the USA has proven to be extremely fraught. The status quo is far too slow and far too expensive.

We decided to tackle this problem and began developing a fully integrated production system that allows us to validate orders, move materials, manage quality and assemble PCBs in single-unit quantities while pushing turn times from weeks to hours and prices down significantly.
